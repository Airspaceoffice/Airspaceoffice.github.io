The Airspace Office App lets office administrators understand their employee needs and adjust office services based on these actionable insights.

Reserve Rooms & Desks

Browse the availability of all conference rooms and hot desks easily.

Invite colleagues to conference room reservations, and add the reservation to your calendar.

Submit Service Requests

Submit service requests around the office in just a few taps.

The auto-routing feature automatically notifies the appropriate office administrators whenever a request is created. For example, all IT requests can automatically go to the office's IT specialist.

Register Guests

Register guests before they visit, so the office receptionist is ready for them. The app will send an email to the guest with details & directions to the office.

Guests can even self check-in from the same email when they arrive, and their host will be notified.
