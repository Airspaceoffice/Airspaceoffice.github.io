Experience Manager Benefits

Quality Assurance

Your EM takes the time to check that your office is operating at its best without you having to think about it. With routine check-ins that look at everything from air quality and office supply needs to conference rooms connectivity.

Operational Support

Whether you have a dedicated office manager or not, your Airspace Experience Manager is your go to for questions and requests.

Cost Reducing Expertise

No matter if you need to scale your IT network or to add new amenities, your EM helps manage the transition and connects you to vetted and trusted vendor and service partners.

In addition to cost savings as a result of efficiency gains, Airspace receives volume discounts from major hardware, software, and service providers. All while reducing vetting and monitoring costs.

Experience Managers save you time by both finding issues before they escalate and by stream-lining the vetting, scheduling, and monitoring of services and vendors.

FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

  • For Companies
  • For Landlords

Are there hidden fees?

No hidden fees. No need to purchase any other service. Free means free.


Who is my Experience Manager?

Our EMs are office space experts hand-picked for your office. This means that you have one point of contact, with a real name (not a call center) that visits your office frequently and that you can call, text, or email with questions and requests.


Our industry has large privacy concerns. How can we be sure that our privacy is protected?

The EM does not look at or into any business specific documents or archives. Their number one concern is the general office space and its operations. Additionally, we have a very company friendly service terms that ensures your privacy is always respected.

Do you work with Landlords?

Landlords can offer Experience Managers to their tenants. In addition, most landlords combine our Office App, which offers many office specific as well as building specific solutions, to create a customer service focused tenant experience. To learn more visit the Landlord Partnership Page.


Do you have a broker representing you? Can I?

We are always open to developing strategic partnerships and opportunities. Tell us more about the property you have in mind.
