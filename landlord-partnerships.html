Is Airspace a Co-working space?

Airspace is not a co-working space. Airspace brings customer focused services and technology to private offices directly and on behalf of landlord partners.

Do I have to adopt the Office App and Experience Management Services together?

While you are not required to adopt the application and services together, we highly recommend you do. The two work well together to generate insights unmatched by any other platform or service.

Case Study: Tenant Employee puts in a service request that identifies broken office furniture. The office admin receives the request simultaneously with the Experience Manager. The Experience Manager contacts the Admin to provide support and access to the Airspace network of vetted and trusted service partners who provide assistance at volume discount prices. The EM then manages the process and even follows up to make sure everything was completed in a timely and satisfactory manner.

How much does it cost for me to add Airspace office App and experience management to my building?

Pricing depends on your property’s specification. Contact us to get an accurate quote and information on how we ensure a successful launch.

How do I know if my building is a good fit for Airspace?

We don't have a one-size-fits-all mentality: our portfolio is comprised of many different types of properties and workspaces. Send us your building information and we'll get back to you ASAP.

How do your services fit into our existing management and leasing structure?

We have a one team approach when it comes to your property. Your existing management company manages the building while we manage the office specific amenities and technology. We work closely with your leasing representatives to attract the tenants that best meet your building needs.
