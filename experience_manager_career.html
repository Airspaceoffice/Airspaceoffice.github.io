Experience Manager

What we're looking for:

We are looking for a full-time experience manager for our Chicago office. You will be focusing on managing service and amenities for our client's private offices. You'll be an integral member of our operations team and report to our C)O.

As an Airspace Experience Manager you will be tasked with managing the administrative duties of our clients offices (Inventory Management, Managing Services, Collecting Feedback, Actively Resolving Issues, On-boarding offices, Communicating with third-party vendors, Project Management, Etc.)

Relevant Skills & Experience:

  • Office Management or hospitality experience perferred
  • Strong time management
  • Self-starter with an entreprenurial outlook
  • Experience managing others
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

Submit a resume to Justin.Kelley@airspaceoffice.co. Next, we'll interview you in person and you can meet the team. We want to know how you're planning to approach this position (i.e., your goals).
