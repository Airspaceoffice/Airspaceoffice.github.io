FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • General
  • The Space

Is Airspace a Co-working space?

Airspace is not a co-working space. Airspace designs, builds, and services semi-private offices on flexible terms.

What type of tenants do you attract?

Our cutting-edge designs combine with our obsession for world-class amenities attract a range of tenants from small startups to enterprise flagships

Do I have to lease my space starting at year long terms?

You are not limited on the length of terms you want for your space. You can choose to lease your Airspace designed and serviced space on traditional terms. While many of our tenants look for terms starting at a year, a large number demand our spaces because of their amenities and turn-key nature and are open to longer terms.

Does Airspace fill the spaces it designs and services?

The goal of Airspace is to create a network of turn-key, serviced offices across the country that we can match up to our growing tenant demand.

How do I know if my building is a good fit for Airspace?

We don't have a one-size-fits-all mentality: our portfolio is comprised of many different types of properties and workspaces. Send us your building information and we'll get back to you ASAP.

How much will building the space cost?

While design and renovation costs can differ based on your building, floor, and market, you can expect to pay 15% less on our spaces than a comparable traditional office. This 15% is the aggregate of the 21% savings on hard costs and materials and the slightly higher costs associated with furniture, audio/visual, and technology. Ultimately, you will have a space that costs less to create and that also saves you going forward on on-going renovation and tenant improvements costs.

How does the partnership agreement work?

Our typical partnership deal structure involves the design and management of office spaces within your commercial building.

How do your services fit into our existing management and leasing structure?

Your existing management company manages the building while we manage the floors design and amenities. We work closely with your leasing representatives to attract the tenants that best meet your building needs.
