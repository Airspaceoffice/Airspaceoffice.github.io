FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Airspace a Co-working space?

Airspace is not a co-working space. Airspace designs, builds, and services semi-private offices on flexible terms.

What type of tenants do you attract?

Airspace is known for creating spaces that exude a professional athmosphere within turn-key spaces that offer amazing amenities. That is why we are able to attract tenants that range from small law firms and marketing firms to large fortune 500 companies.

How do I know if my building is a good fit for Airspace?

We don't have a one-size-fits-all mentality: our portfolio is comprised of many different types of properties and workspaces. Send us your building information and we'll get back to you ASAP.

How does the partnership agreement work?

Our typical partnership deal structure involves the design and management of office spaces within your commercial building.

How do your services fit into our existing management and leasing structure?

Your existing management company manages the building while we manage the floors design and amenities. We work closely with your leasing representatives to attract the tenants that best meet your building needs.
