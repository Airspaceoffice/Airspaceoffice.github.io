FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • General
  • The Space

Is Airspace a Co-working space?

Airspace is not a co-working space. Airspace services and tech-enables semi-private and private offices.

What type of tenants do you attract?

Our obsession with world-class amenities attract a range of tenants from small startups to enterprise flagships looking for their next HQ.

Do I have to lease my space starting at year long terms?

You are not limited on the length of terms you want for your space. You can choose to lease your office Powered by Airspace on traditional terms. While many of our tenants look for terms starting at a year, a large number demand our spaces because of their amenities and all-inclusive nature and are open to longer terms.

Does Airspace fill the spaces it designs and services?

The goal of Airspace is to create a network of all-inclusive, serviced offices across the country that we can match up to our growing tenant demand.

How do I know if my building is a good fit for Airspace?

We don't have a one-size-fits-all mentality: our portfolio is comprised of many different types of properties and workspaces. Send us your building information and we'll get back to you ASAP.

If I want to design and build a space that then can be Powered by Airspace, how much might that cost?

While design and renovation costs can differ based on your building, floor, and market, you can expect to pay 15% less on modular spaces than a comparable traditional office. This 15% is the aggregate of the 21% savings on hard costs and materials and the slightly higher costs associated with furniture, audio/visual, and technology. Ultimately, you will have a space that costs less to create and that also saves you going forward on on-going renovation and tenant improvements costs.

How does the partnership agreement work?

Our typical partnership deal structure involves the amenity servicing and technology enablement of the office spaces in your property. Our service and technology costs can be passed through to the tenant with premiums generated by your new offering staying with you.

How do your services fit into our existing management and leasing structure?

We have a one team approach when it comes to your property. Your existing management company manages the building while we manage the office specific amenities and technology. We work closely with your leasing representatives to attract the tenants that best meet your building needs.
