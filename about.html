About Us

Who We Are

Founded by a University of Chicago students in 2018, Airspace is built around the idea that space is not a binary concept revolving around whether you have it or not. Our Services and products aim to increase the efficiency and utilization of space while creating environments that foster growth, collaboration, and innovation.

What We Do

Airspace partners with some of the most unique and recognizable office buildings across the country to provide turn-Key, flexible Spaces that give business the amenities they deserve without sacrificing the space, professionalism, and price-point they need. Business looking to expand quickly, scale staffing, or protect their business from market volatility can do so easily with Airspace offices and enterprise solutions.
