Software Engineer, Web Dev
Chicago, IL

What we're looking for:

We are looking for a full-time software engineer for the Airspace Chicago office. We want you to help us design, build and maintain the next generation of Airspace's internal web tools and space management platform. You'll be an integral member of our engineering team and report to our CTO.

Relevant Skills:

  • Knowledge of algorithms and data structures (Python, Java, or equivalent)
  • Previous experience building responsive web applications & knowledge of React, Angular, Vue or any other front-end framework
  • Clear communication and ability to own projects end-to-end & ready to hit the ground running with a commit on day one
  • Familiarity with relational or non-relational databases (we use NoSQL databases)
  • Experience in any backend language (we use Node.js)

What you'll get out of this:

  • Technical Skills: Our team has technical members that will help you grow your technical chops.
  • Soft Skills: We strongly believe very member of our team should be able to sell the Airspace experience. Throughout your time with us, you'll be working alongside our team of UChicago Law & Booth students to help you refine your soft skills.

What's the interview process like?

Submit a portfolio with your resume to aditya.gunda@airspaceoffice.co. Your resume can be just a few lines with your experiences. If you have a portfolio or repositories of past projects, be sure to include links to them. Next, we'll interview you in person and you can meet the team. We want to know how you're planning to approach this position (i.e., your goals). This is also the time for you to ask us any questions and gauge a fit. We will respond to everyone that responds to us so please don't hesitate to reach out!
