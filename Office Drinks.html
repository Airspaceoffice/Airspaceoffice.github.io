Kegs Serve More Than Booze Now: 5 Alternatives To Beer In The Office
November 9, 2019 by Justin K.

Kegs are great way to add a new perk to your office at an affordable cost. What’s more, kegs today are far more advance than the beer kegs from your college days. Choices are varied. Prices are affordable. And there are a number of partners like KegNation or Limitless, that can deliver the kegs and equipment right to your office for a straightforward and affordable price.

1. Cold Brew Coffee
When it comes to caffeine levels, cold brew packs a heavier punch than hot coffee. Heightened caffeine levels are one major reason that cold brew coffee is especially popular among office workers, helping them to stay alert and focused. What’s more, there are a number of innovative brewers like Big Shoulders and Stumptown Coffee Roasters, that are pioneering new flavors and formulas to keep your office buzzing.

2. Nitro Cold Brew
Like regular Cold Brew Coffee, Nitro is a drink that packs a caffeine punch, but has a much smoother and creamier mouthfeel. That’s because Nitro is infused with tiny nitrogen bubbles, and the bubbles give this coffee a foamy texture, like a stout beer. Companies like KegNation work directly with suppliers to deliver this delicious nectar to offices at transparent and fair subscriptions.

3. Kombucha
Kombucha is a fermented tea that has been consumed for thousands of years, the health benefits of which have been widely touted. Although the exact origins of the drink are unknown, luckily for offices there are a number of innovators, like Whalebird and Health Aide, supplying kombucha kegs across the nation.

4. Nitro Matcha Tea
Many may already be familiar with the unique taste and health benefits of matcha green tea. Nitro Matcha Tea elevates the experience by giving the celebrated flavor profile a natural creaminess and savory mouthfeel, much like Nitro Cold Brew. The result is a drink full of antioxidants that regulates caffeine levels for a feeling of calm alertness that spans hours. Companies like Limitless have led the charge on this drink, and others are quickly elevating the game.

5. Iced Tea With the innovation in keg drinks, iced tea is no exception. From estate-grown Ceylon tea leaves to lively, tart and fruity blends of floral flavors, there’s enough to keep everyone happy. Best served over ice, iced tea kegs are celebrated as an alternative drink for those that prefer a lower/non-caffeinated beverage. Connect with a service like KegNation to setup dual taps of Nitro Cold Brew and Iced Tea!
