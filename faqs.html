General FAQS

Is Airspace a Co-working space?

Airspace is not a co-working space. Airspace designs, builds, and services semi-private offices on flexible terms.


What is The Cost of a Space? What is included?

The pricing includes designing, building out, and servicing your office space. This also includes the space, furniture, branding, Wi-Fi, utilities, cleaning, receptionist, coffee, stocked kitchen, and office space management software solutions.


How do you provide all these amenities and services while keeping the space affordable?

A few things help us keep costs for you low. Our modular space designs are customizable without requiring complete re-designs specific to each space, saving on buildout costs. Our combination of private, semi-private, and shared open spaces allows organizations to share traditional costs, saving in the absolute on the space.

Space FAQs

Do you own the property?

Airspace does not own the properties. We act as a design and management service to pre-screened and hand-selected spaces that provide high value to our tenants.


How long will it take for us to move in?

After the consultation, we begin working on finding the perfect space and other organizations that will create space synergy. Depending on the space that is chosen, an existing Airspace office can be moved into almost immediately as available while other locations can take 2-3 months.

Can you build conference rooms if we need more?

Yes! We are experts in space management and because our designs are modular, the space can be altered as your needs change.

Broker FAQs

How does Airspace work with brokers - aren't they doing the same thing?

Airspace relies on brokers to help their clients understand the value proposition of flexible terms, and determine if Airspace is an ideal option for their needs. From there, we're with you every step of the way, and you can rely on our infrastructure to help you delight your clients. Consider us your strategic partner.


Can I tour my client tomorrow?

We'd love the opportunity to make an impression on your client tomorrow. The first step of our process is to have a conversation with you and learn how your client's needs can be uniquely satisfied by Airspace. If you are in a time crunch, not to worry, our deal cycles are often accelerated and take merely weeks.


Do you pay commission?

Yes. Review our commission structure here.


Do you only do short-term agreements?

Though we tend to focus on short-term agreements, we are open to longer term agreements as well. We also include an additional broker commission upon renewal, regardless of the agreement length.
